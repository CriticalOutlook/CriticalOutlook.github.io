Welcome!

This is the official website for the Critical Outlook podcast.

Run by Pranav Eranki and Ben Hayum, this podcast aims to cover issues critical to the betterment of society.

We will incorporate the expertise and perspectives of professors, researchers, entrepreneurs, activists, club founders, and more into these conversations. When discussing with them, we will focus on getting a genuine look into their personal philosophy and examining the work that they have dedicated themselves to.

The main topics we hope to cover are tech, sciences, sustainability, future scenarios, philosophy, ethics/morality, mental wellbeing, self-development, social climate, community, and varying political issues.

Most importantly, we will ensure that equity of voice, respect, and truth are at the forefront of this podcast’s focus, as we do not want to abate genuine discourse or diversity of opinion. Through all of this, we hope to be entertaining, informative, and effectively look to the future while transforming information into actionable insights for you.

We're looking forward to what we, as a community, can learn and achieve together! We hope to be very transparent with our work, even to a fault. You can interact with us in our Discord, telling us what topics you want to see, help us brainstorm, and can even call us out when we make a mistake! We don't want to neglect anything, and are always open to constructive criticism. Help us help you.

Check out our community engagement section for more info on our socials and Discord.

Cheers,

Discord

Our discord is our main way of staying in touch with you guys, and for you to discuss amongst yourselves. Here, you can have conversations with other listeners regarding existing podcasts and potential future ones, tell us what topics you would like to see, and critique us on our style and/or knowledge. We will be hosting Discord-only scheduled creativer sessions where we plan to brainstorm ways to improve the podcast, or use the knowledge of our fanbase to look deeper into specific topics. We want to provide the best info possible, and we can do that by actively factoring in your feedback and criticism. Please join our Discord and contribute back to the podcast!
