Welcome!

This is the official website for the Critical Outlook podcast.

Run by Pranav Eranki and Ben Hayum, this podcast aims to cover issues critical to the betterment of society.

We will incorporate the expertise and perspectives of professors, researchers, entrepreneurs, activists, club founders, and more into these conversations. When discussing with them, we will focus on getting a genuine look into their personal philosophy and examining the work that they have dedicated themselves to.

The main topics we hope to cover are tech, sciences, sustainability, future scenarios, philosophy, ethics/morality, mental wellbeing, self-development, social climate, community, and varying political issues.

Most importantly, we will ensure that equity of voice, respect, and truth are at the forefront of this podcastâ€™s focus, as we do not want to abate genuine discourse or diversity of opinion. Through all of this, we hope to be entertaining, informative, and effectively look to the future while transforming information into actionable insights for you.

We're looking forward to what we, as a community, can learn and achieve together! We hope to be very transparent with our work, even to a fault. You can interact with us in our Discord, telling us what topics you want to see, help us brainstorm, and can even call us out when we make a mistake! We don't want to neglect anything, and are always open to constructive criticism. Help us help you.

Check out our socials and join our discord for more.
